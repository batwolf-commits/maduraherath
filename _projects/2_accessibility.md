*Mälardalen University Library · Jan 2025 – Mar 2025*

Led a user-centered design project to improve the accessibility and usability of the Mälardalen University Library website for students with visual impairments and dyslexia. Through expert interviews, target group interviews, workshops, and usability testing, the team identified key barriers in navigation, search, and assistive technology compatibility, then developed and tested a prototype addressing them.

**Outcomes**

- Reduced cognitive load through simplified navigation and layout
- Enhanced search efficiency via redesigned filters and AI-powered suggestions
- Integrated support for screen readers and keyboard navigation
- Positive user feedback confirming improved usability and inclusiveness
