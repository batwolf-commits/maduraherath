*SLIIT · Jun 2023 – Jul 2023*

Designed and implemented a SQL-based data warehouse to centralize restaurant operations data and support advanced analytics. Created a star schema model for efficient querying across time, menu items, customers, and locations, with ETL processes extracting data from POS systems on a scheduled basis.

**Outcomes**

- Improved query performance by 40% through optimized indexing and partitioning
- Enabled analysis of over 5,000 monthly transactions
- Supported data-driven decisions across marketing, procurement, and operations
