*Mälardalen University · Sep 2024 – Jan 2025*

A mobile app concept promoting sustainable living through local tool sharing — borrow and lend tools within communities to reduce waste, cut costs, and build trust. Developed through sustainability-focused workshops with youth participants of Västerås Folkhögskola. Evaluated multiple ideas using Affinity Mapping and the Six Thinking Hats method before selecting ToolShare as the most impactful and practical solution.

**Core features**

- Secure login with personalized profiles
- List, request, and schedule tools with calendar-based availability
- Reviews and ratings to build trust among users
- Geo-based tool discovery on an interactive map
- Borrowing agreements and deposit system for safety
